Rod.Table
A table made from easily found 1” dowel, hardware, and a 3D printed part. While the 3D printed part makes the joint between the table top and the legs, it is also the drill guide for the fabrication of the table. Therefore, this joint/drill guide is conceptually all that is needed to make the table in the form of a 3D print file along with access to a 3D printer.
